Asokoro

Asokoro is one of the most expensive and wealthy areas in the whole country. Known as the residential area for politicians, the property value is extremely high by national standards. Asokoro has everything from shopping centers, to eateries and lounges. It is a diverse residential area, with both vibrant and serene locales.
