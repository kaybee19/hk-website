about

Hassan + King Real Estate Consulting

Founded in 2015 by Mubarak Hassan and Osita King, Hassan + King has grown into one of the premier real estate consulting service in Nigeria. Headquartered in Abuja, we are strategically located to effectively manage our diverse portfolio of clients and partners.

Central Mosque, Abuja

Our leaders

mubarak hassan
Founder and Managing Director

Mubarak Hassan closed his first real estate deal in 1995 at the young age of 22. He has since gone on to sell properties and oversee deals worth billions of naira. The son of a realtor, Mubarak majored in real estate and received his MBA from University of Toronto.


He has over 20 years of experience in estate management and development, having dealt with some of the largest property developers in Abuja. He has secured partnerships with big names like Brains and Hammers and Urban Shelter.


Mubarak Hassan serves on the board of AEAN (Association of Estate Agents in Nigeria) and is active in pursing the standardization of estate agency practice across Nigeria.


osita king
Co-Founder and Director

Osita King is one of the hottest names in real estate in Abuja today. An expert appraiser, he worked with AGIS (Abuja Geographical Information Systems) as a senior project manager.


A graduate of Covenant University, he has over 10 years of experience in appraisals and securing property contracts. Leveraging on his large network of contacts, he has secured partnerships with numerous developers all across Abuja including Cosgrove Smart Estate.


With Mr. King's wide skills and expertise, Hasssan + King has diversified into licensing, international consultations, and a broader spectrum of real estate services.
